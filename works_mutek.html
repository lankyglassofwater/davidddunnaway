MUTEK is non-profit organization dedicated to the dissemination and development of digital creativity in sound, music, and audio-visual art. Applying their mission statement and available references from past events to create a visual campaign for a festival series, Pangea.online was initiated. The event is a homage to each of their major global branches, uniting each in a digital utopia where artists from any part of the world showcase their art.
