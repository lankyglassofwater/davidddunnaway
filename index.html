Hello, my name is David Dunnaway. I am deeply inspired by music and contemporary design. I am fascinated by the intersection of fine art and graphic design. The movement of art in this accelerated era of technology is terrifying but exciting, and I hope to imbue this feeling with an empathetic touch of my current personal obsessions.
We live in an ephemeral and liminal space. Constantly updating, upgrading, and downsizing. Minimal and maximal. Nothing is permanent and everything lasts forever. Let's make art.
